Leonel Gonzalez Visual Artist

Leonel Gonzalez is a Venezuelan visual artist whose hyperrealist works are known for technical precision and emotional depth. His passion for horses and landscapes is reflected in every brushstroke, forging a powerful visual connection between the viewer and subject. He has participated in national and international exhibitions and is celebrated for his mastery of detail and composition. His work is a constant exploration of beauty, movement, and strength.
