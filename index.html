About the Artist

Leonel Gonzalez is a Venezuelan visual artist currently based in New York. His paintings center around hyperrealistic textures, organic elements, and fungi-inspired forms. His work captures nature’s raw elegance, often blending detail, mood, and decay in ways that provoke reflection and reverence.
